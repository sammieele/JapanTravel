DISCOVER

Tokyo

As the capital of Japan, Tokyo is filled with cherry blossoms, temples and numerous subway stations that make it a popular city for visitors. Immersing yourself in Tokyo is the best way to experience the city.

Kyoto

Famous for its preserved temples and tranquil gardens, Kyoto is home to Japan’s rich culture and history. Traditional houses line the winding streets and tatami rooms are not uncommon.

Osaka

A neon city bustling with life, Osaka is a fast-paced city offering many opportunities for food and shopping entertainment. Street food is most popular in Osaka for low prices.
