About Japan

Famous for its rich cultural offerings and innovation, Japan has a variety of countless activities for you to explore from the breathtaking Shibuya Skyline to the bustling Ameyoko Market.

While Japan is particularly popular in the spring, Japan hosts Lunar New Year celebrations at the turn of the season, and warm Okinawan beaches in the summer.
